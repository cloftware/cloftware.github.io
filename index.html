Questions

Things people usually ask.

Which time zones do you work across?

Our hours overlap with US, UK, and European working days, in addition to IST — so stand-ups and reviews don't need to wait until tomorrow.

Do you work with an existing codebase?

Yes. A lot of our work is picking up projects mid-flight — auditing what's there, then fixing, extending, or migrating it without a full rewrite.

How is a project priced?

Depends on the engagement model you pick: fixed price for defined scopes, monthly for a dedicated team, or hourly for ongoing support. We'll recommend one after the discovery call.

Can you handle both the product and its infrastructure?

Yes — that's the point of one team covering full-stack. We design the interface, build the backend, and set up the cloud infrastructure it all runs on.

What happens after launch?

We stay on. Most clients move to hourly support or a smaller retainer for fixes, monitoring, and the features that only show up once real users arrive.
